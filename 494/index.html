Custom Mechanic: The Omnitrix from Ben10 which allows link to transform into slain bosses and make use of their unique abilities.


Contributions: For this custom level, I designed three of the boss fights, movement and attack mechanics for 2 different player form mutations, and created half of the custom dungeon.


This project was completed using C# scripting in Unity.
